Not every site needs a concrete shelter — but every site needs one that lasts.
That's the thinking behind the **Datacomm Pro Series**: a lightweight shelter line
engineered telecom-tough for operators, carriers, and edge data providers.

## Lightweight, without the compromise

Compared to concrete shelters, the Datacomm Pro Series offers savings of **up to
45%**, while weighing substantially less and keeping the structural integrity that
critical communications and data infrastructure require.

## Built to take it

Each shelter is built on reinforced 2x4 framing, clad internally with ½-inch
FRP/OSB panels, and finished with ½-inch wall sheathing, a ¾-inch exterior-grade
plywood roof deck, and durable 5/16-inch cement-board siding. Every unit sits on
galvanized steel skids for easy deployment — and it's proudly made in the USA.

- Six standard sizes up to **23' x 36'**, supporting up to **50 racks**
- Rated for **150 MPH winds** and **90 psf snow loads**, seismic-rated construction
- Dual 3–10 ton HVAC options with available hot/cold aisle containment

## When you need more

For extreme environments, the **ULTRA Series** steps up to 2"x6" framing rated for
winds up to 180 MPH, and Enhanced Ballistics Protection upgrades deliver up to
Level 3 resistance against forced entry, vandalism, and firearm threats.
