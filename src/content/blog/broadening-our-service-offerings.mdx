CellSite Solutions started in 2009 by remanufacturing telecom equipment shelters —
a more sustainable, cost-effective alternative to building new. Following a 2021
investment from Fort Point Capital, we've grown well beyond the warehouse.

## A complete turnkey provider

Today we build teams of electricians, HVAC technicians, mechanical engineers, and
civil engineers to manage entire projects. That means we now handle everything
from shelter and generator foundations, grounding, electrical work, and compound
fencing to backup generators, asset disposal, equipment removal, and complete site
decommissioning.

## What that means for our customers

You get one accountable partner across the full life cycle of a cell site:

- **[Civil construction](/civil-construction)** — foundations, grounding, trenching, and fencing
- **[Installation & integration](/installation-integration)** — equipment installed, connected, and running
- **[Generators](/generators)** and **[HVAC](/hvac-units)** — reliable power and cooling
- **[General maintenance](/general-site-maintenance)** — keeping sites at peak performance
- **[Decommissioning](/site-decommissioning)** — responsible teardown and restoration

From a single site to a multi-regional deployment, we bring the trades and the
inventory together — so you don't have to manage a chain of subcontractors.
